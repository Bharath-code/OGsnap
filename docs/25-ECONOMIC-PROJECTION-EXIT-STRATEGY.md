## PART 2: Exit Strategy

### Option 1: Bootstrap Forever (Most Likely)

**Why it works:**
- Profitable from month 1
- No VC dilution
- Full control
- Lifestyle business potential

**The Reality:**
- $175K MRR = $2.1M ARR
- Take $500K-1M salary/year
- Sell services around it
- Very comfortable living

**Exit Multiple:** N/A (never sell)

---

### Option 2: Strategic Acquisition

**Who might buy:**

| Potential Acquirer | Rationale | Estimated Value |
|-------------------|-----------|----------------|
| Vercel | Eliminate OG competitor, add to platform | $2-5M |
| Cloudflare | Add to Workers/R2 ecosystem | $3-6M |
| Netlify | Complete their image offering | $1-3M |
| Cloudinary | Add dynamic OG to their product | $2-4M |
| Shopify | OG for merchant sites | $5-10M |

**What they'd pay:**
- 5-10x ARR
- At $2M ARR â†’ $10-20M potential

**Timeline:** Year 3-5

---

### Option 3: Raise VC (Only If)

**When to raise:**
- Framework partnerships secured (proved distribution)
- $500K+ MRR (proved product-market fit)
- Competitor acquiring similar space

**What you'd give up:**
- 20-25% for $2-5M
- Board seats
- Growth pressure

**Verdict:** Probably not worth it for this business

### The "Never Forget" Principles

1. **Support responds within 24 hours**
   - Even if just "looking into it"
   
2. **Every bug is someone's blocked project**
   - Treat with urgency
   
3. **Free users are tomorrow's paying customers**
   - Don't neglect them
   
4. **Simplicity > Features**
   - 3 great features > 10 mediocre ones
   
5. **Communicate proactively**
   - Tell users about issues before they discover them
   - Share roadmap, ask for input
